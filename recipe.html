Cucumber Salad

Lucias Special Sauce turned into numbers

Ready in: 15–20 min

Ingredients

  • 2 mini cucumbers
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tsp sesame oil
  • 4 tbsp black vinegar
  • 3-4 tsp chili oil (can vary with spice want)
  • 2 tsp sugar
Instacart Includes: cucumbers, rice vinegar, black vineagar, chili oil, sugar (i assumed you had salt and soy sauce)

Steps

  1. Slice cucumbers! (thinner is better)
  2. In a tupperware, mix dressing: rice vinegar + black vinegar + soy sauce + sesame oil +chili oil
  3. Toss cucumbers in dressing until well coated and super yummy
  4. Optional: let it soak 10 minutes to soak up the flavor

Serving ideas

Our favorites: scallion pancakes, buldak, or tomato soup
